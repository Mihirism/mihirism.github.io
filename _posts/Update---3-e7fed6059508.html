Update — 3

We completed our teacher training program at Vapi and now this is the 2nd week of my journey as Teaching fellow at Baxi Panch Ashram Shala — Primary School run by Muni Seva Ashram, Goraj.

Important news and updates:

  • Jubin Maheta visited our school and ashram on 15th June.
  • we are implementing ‘step up’(inspired by multi grade multi level program from rishi valley school.) program for std 1st to 4th at our school
  • we are ready with open library for our kids
  • we are ready with science, social sci and English corners for our kids to give hands on learning experience.
  • Got useful children books from Sashwat Ratna — Madhya Pradesh.
  • Got the educational games donated by Madrat Games.
  • we completed school admission program on 20th June.

Upcoming Programs and ideas:

  • we are organizing wikipedia meet up at our school on 27th June 2015. Agenda: How to use wikipedia in education , How to contribute to wikipedia.
  • we are planning to start event like English week where kids can learn various topic of English language with fun and practical approach.
  • evening learning circle and other extra activities…

I have few more ideas in the mind but I have to craft it. I will keep you updated.
