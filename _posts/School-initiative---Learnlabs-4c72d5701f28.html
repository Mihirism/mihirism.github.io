School initiative — Learnlabs

in the first phase of our project we are going to work at Sharda Mandir School — supported by Muni Seva Ashram, Vadodra. For more details please visit our website www.learnlabs.in .

what we will do to develop our school as Model school?

There will be two part : 1st — kindergarten to std 4th, 2nd — std 5 to 8th.

1st part : we are going to implement ‘step up’ program adopted from Maa Foundation, Vapi.(will share detail information in individual post).

2nd part: we will develop 1. open library 2. sci/maker lab(we have DIY kits for according to curriculum of std 6th to 8th) 3. curriculum and system development for computer lab (we have 21 computers made with N-Computing devices, 1 TB central Hard Disk with educational resources and video tutorials for std 6th to 8th , Projector, sound system, whit board, 1 laptop). 4. development of sci, math, map, junk, etc. activity corners. 5. development of sci-math clubs. 6. development of curriculum and innovative methods for English language improvement. 7. other activites like ram-hat , khoya-paya 8. overall educational and oprations direction.
