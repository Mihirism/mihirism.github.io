Why-What and How -learnlabs

Why?

The ultimate goal of human life is to marge with nature.

It will only happen when human can know their self, know his/her real identity

Education is the very important tool to get that stage of self realization.

But what can we do about today’s education system!!

Current scenario:

there are 1000s of people trying to improve education system in different ways.

by making ipad apps, smart class, DIY kits, Experimental learning, life skills via sports and computers, etc. some IITan started own schools…

communities of alternative education and home schoolers, brain researchers, child psychologist are trying solve this problem with their own way. and yes our government is also trying to do something…

Now What should I do ?

I was failed to know my inner call because of this education system, so now I have to contribute my self to improve this.

I started my journey:

I went back to my school at village and started exploring real grass root problems at school.

  • thr are no computers in so called computer lab
  • no qualified sci-math teachers
  • no equipments for sci experiments
  • no educational resource to learn English
  • rout learning and exams oriented learning
  • no real life or application based learning

I started studying different educational models from different countries, visited alternative education centers, met 100s of educators…

studied project based, application based, experimental learning methods.

after these I realize that ‘child’ is capable to learn themselves, but we only need to give environment and opportunity to learn.

How?

finally I decided to make that school(where we started) as model school with innovative teaching-learning methods. child centric school, learner centric and learner driven school.

I started making modules and collecting resources for this Dream Project. I planned to develop that school as lab for our experiments — experimental school. we will spread our best practices to the other schools.

we connected our selves with different communities, working to improve education like home schoolers, alternative school owners, educators, experimental learning promoters, digital content maker, engineers, designers and operations experts.

to make this dream project live, I met Lakhan Samani. he helped me to make website for project (now it’s called ‘learn labs’, we finalize the name after brainstorming.)now he is leading every technical development for good at learnlabs. I convinced few senior people from USA and UK to join us as director and guide us to make it happen. Mr. Narendra Phanse(ex army man) and Suresh Jani(ex engineer) helped with initial funding and guidances. our school is located at village called ‘Goraj’ near by Vadodra, this school is supported by NGO called Muni Seva Ashram.

so this is what it is..
