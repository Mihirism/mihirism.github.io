The Book of Awesomeness!!

Currently I am translating one FADOO book call ‘The Book of Awesomeness!!’. This book is written by Sohil Patel(The Robotics Geek) and Nirman Dave. They both are members of FabLab Ahmadabad.

This book is based on ‘Papertronics’(Experiments of electronics with the help of paper and pencil), we can replace copper wire and circuit board with pencil and paper to create awesome circuits. In this book there are few experiments based on this concept.

I think this book is very useful to explore basic physic and electronics for primary school’s students. so that I am translating this book in Gujarati, so students from my village can understand easily and start exploring this science in fun way…

Thank you very much to Sohil Patel for introducing such Awesome idea…
